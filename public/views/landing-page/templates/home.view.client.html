About Pokémon World

What is Pokémon World ?

Pokémon World is our attempt to emulate the hugely popular Pokémon Universe on the web. We are fan boys of the franchise and this is our tribute to it. We created this as a final project for CS5610 course and do not intend to use it for commercial purposes

Original plan includes creation of the Pokémon Trading Card Game which emulates Pokémon fights between two trainers. We also created a Pokédex module which emulates the Pokédex from the game.

Future Scope: We intend to implement Gym Battles and two-player mode.

Inspirations for this project

We would like to thank Satoshi Tajiri - creator of the franchise without which our childhood's would not have been so interesting.

This project could not have been possible without the existence of some amazing API's. We have tremendous gratitude for creators of these API's and keeping it open for educational purposes.

Pokémon TCG API : Provides information on cards and sets

Poké API: PokéAPI is primarily an educational tool and is free and open to use. It provides all data related to the franchise and is well documented.

Pokémon website: We were heavily inspired by Pokémon's original website.

Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG)

Play the Game!

Pokémon Trading Card Game is our attempt to create a web version of the hugely popular Pokémon Trading Card Game using MEAN Stack.

The basic game play remains similar to the original game. Players assume the role of a Pokémon trainer and use their Pokémon to battle their opponents. In our game the opponent is the computer which makes its moves automatically.

To keep the game fair, the cards are assigned at random. Every attack reduces the Hit Points(HP) of the opponents Pokémon. A Pokémon gets knocked off if it's HP falls below 0.

The player wins when all if the opponents Pokémons are knocked off. A card is gifted to the winner and added to his profile.

What is Pokédex?

Pokédex The Pokédex is an electronic device designed to catalogue and provide information regarding the various species of Pokémon featured in the Pokémon video game, anime and manga series.

The name Pokédex is a neologism including "Pokémon" (which itself is a portmanteau of "pocket" and "monster") and "index". The Japanese name is simply "Pokémon Encyclopedia", and it can feature every Pokémon on it, depending on the Pokédex.

Every Pokémon trainer has a Pokédex for identifying Pokémons they come across in their quest to catch 'em all.

Gym & Gym Battles

A Gym is a place where Pokémon Trainers go to train their Pokémon. Just like real-life gymnasiums, where one can go to work out and build physical strength, Pokémon Gyms are places where Trainers can go to sharpen their battling skills and where their Pokémon can go to gain experience. Often specializing in a particular type, Gyms create an environment which allows Trainers to test both their skills and Pokémon against those of others.

The most powerful Trainer in a given Gym is called the Gym Leader, who is revered by both the lower-ranking members of the Gym and local fans.

We are working on bringing this feature to Pokémon World very soon.
